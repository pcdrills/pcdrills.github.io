## Introduction
> Are you tired of constant group notifications on WhatsApp? Do you want to silence a particular group without leaving it?
{: .prompt-tip}

---

Here's how to mute group notifications:

## Procedure
Steps to Mute Group Notifications
1. *Open WhatsApp* and go to the group chat you wish to mute.
2. Tap the *group name* at the top.
3. Click on notifications
4. Select *"Mute"* or *"Mute notifications"*.
5. Choose the duration:
    - 8 hours
    - 1 week
    - 1 year
    - Always (to mute indefinitely)
![mute_notifications.jpg](https://i.postimg.cc/ZY3BhgnH/mute_notifications.jpg)

6. Alternatively, select *"Custom Notifications"* and choose your preferred settings based on what is available at the moment, Whatsapp is constantly updating.

---

## Conclusions
> This procedure can be very handy in a class group, family group or friends group where you just want to temporary mute notifications at peak dicussion periods where everyone is sending in messages at a moment you don't want to be disturbed.
