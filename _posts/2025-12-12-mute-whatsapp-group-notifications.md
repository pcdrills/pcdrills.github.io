## Procedure
*Mute Group Notifications on WhatsApp: A Step-by-Step Guide*

Are you tired of constant group notifications on WhatsApp? Do you want to silence a particular group without leaving it? Here's how to mute group notifications:

*Steps to Mute Group Notifications*
1. *Open WhatsApp* and go to the group chat.
2. Tap the *group name* at the top.
3. Select *"Mute"* or *"Mute notifications"*.
4. Choose the duration:
    - 8 hours
    - 1 week
    - 1 year
    - Always (to mute indefinitely)
5. Alternatively, select *"Custom Notifications"* and choose your preferred settings.
